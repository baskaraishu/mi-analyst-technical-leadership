# Case Study: Driving MI Excellence with the Leadership Toolkit

This case study shows how the **MI-Leadership-Toolkit** enables a mature, trusted, and business-aligned MI function—covering governance, stakeholder management, data quality, and value delivery.

---

## 1. Business Challenge

The MI team faced:
- Low trust in reports due to unclear KPI definitions and inconsistent data
- Poor stakeholder engagement and low BI adoption
- Slow incident response and lack of auditability
- Difficulty demonstrating MI’s business value

## 2. Solution Overview

The toolkit provides frameworks, templates, and processes to:
- Establish clear data governance and KPI maturity
- Engage stakeholders and improve data literacy
- Systematize data quality checks and incident management
- Track and communicate MI’s business impact
