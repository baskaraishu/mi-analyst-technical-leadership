The **MI Leadership Toolkit** showcases capabilities beyond technical ETL and dashboards. It demonstrates how you approach **data governance, stakeholder management, data quality, and business value** as a senior MI / Analytics leader.

Use this folder as a **portfolio asset** when interviewing for MI, Analytics, or Data leadership roles.

---

## 1. Structure Overview

- **01-Strategic-Data-Governance**  
  Principles, maturity models, and governance artefacts that show how you design a trustworthy MI ecosystem.

- **02-Stakeholder-Operations**  
  Templates and frameworks for gathering requirements, improving data literacy, and managing a report catalogue.

- **03-Data-Quality-Delivery**  
  Checklists, anomaly logs, ETL standards, and RCA templates that prove you can run **reliable MI operations**.

- **04-Business-Value-ROI**  
  Case studies and frameworks for linking MI to **tangible business outcomes**, including GenAI acceleration and BI adoption metrics.

- **05-Practical-Data-Templates**  
  Practical SQL, dashboard wireframes, and audit logs that show how you operationalise good practice.

---

## 2. How to Use This in Interviews

- **Walk recruiters/hiring managers through the folders** to show both your technical literacy and leadership mindset.  
- Use specific files (e.g., `KPI_MATURITY_ROADMAP.md`, `DATA_QUALITY_CHECKLIST.md`, `INSIGHT_TO_ACTION_CASE_STUDY.md`) as **conversation anchors** for how you:
  - Govern KPI definitions and technology.  
  - Manage stakeholders and expectations.  
  - Ensure data quality and incident handling.  
  - Prove business impact and drive adoption.

## 3. Relationship to the Technical Project

This toolkit sits alongside the **crypto price ETL project** in this repo:
